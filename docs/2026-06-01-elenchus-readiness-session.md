## Category A — The adjudication gate (load-bearing)

- [ ] **A1 [CONTRADICTION]** The honesty pact rewards saying "I don't know"; the survival
  criterion ("ready = no I-don't-know") punishes it. The rule is self-opposed and motivates
  bluffing. *Proposed resolution:* "I don't know" is the engine, not the failure. Ready =
  **no unresolved load-bearing unknown + no surviving contradiction/factual error.** The
  council judges consistency & facts, never answer quality. → awaiting author stress-test.
- [ ] **A2 [GAP]** With no judge, what stops a confident bluff from substituting for an honest
  "I don't know"? *Proposed:* contradiction-hunt + fact-check catches most bluffs (they
  betray themselves under follow-up); a perfectly self-consistent confident bluff is the
  irreducible residual the honesty pact must cover. → awaiting author stress-test.
- [ ] **A3 [CONTRADICTION]** Two pass conditions stated: #1 (survive without "I don't know")
  vs #6 (write a consistent build summary). Which is THE gate? *Proposed:* survival is the
  gate; the summary is the artifact that proves it. → awaiting author stress-test.
- [ ] **A4 [GAP]** Who checks the build summary is consistent with the answers without
  re-introducing model-as-judge? *Proposed:* a mechanical contradiction-scan (summary vs.
  logged answers) — same contradiction role, not a quality judgment. → awaiting stress-test.
